# Fastest Engine Known Gaps

Date: 2026-06-28.

This catalog tracks remaining high-value gaps for the v2 fastest-engine work.
It is additive to `docs/performance-acceleration-known-gaps.md`,
`docs/performance-known-gaps.md`, and
`docs/performance-cranelift-known-gaps.md`.

Generated reports under `target/performance/` are local evidence only and must
not be committed.

## Closure Policy

A fastest-engine gap is closed only when the owning work lands its required
source changes or docs, focused fixtures, counters or reports, off switches,
fallback paths, validation evidence, and skip classification for optional tools.
Wall-clock-only evidence cannot close a gap.
